## 📊 Sales Performance & Forecast Dashboard

### 📌 Project Overview

The Sales Performance & Forecast Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to monitor business performance, analyze historical sales trends, and forecast future sales outcomes. The dashboard enables stakeholders to track key performance indicators, identify growth opportunities, evaluate product and regional performance, and make data-driven strategic decisions.

By combining historical sales analysis with forecasting techniques, the dashboard provides valuable insights into future business performance and revenue expectations.

### 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Track revenue and profit trends over time
- Analyze sales by region, product, and category
- Identify top-performing products and markets
- Forecast future sales using historical data
- Support strategic planning and decision-making
